Our Story

Our History & Formation Boston High School–Mpala was founded in 2006 by a seasoned educationist Baguma Gastone and purely professional teachers with great experience from the country’s education system. The school is located on Entebbe road at 1km off Nkumba-Mpala stage in Bubuli Zone. It is situated in an exclusively cool and quiet environment fit for brain concentration. The school has a catholic foundation, however it embraces all religious denomination. The school is a mixed Boarding and Day offering Arts and Sciences at both ‘O’ and ‘A’ Level.
