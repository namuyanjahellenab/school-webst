O-Level Program

The O-Level (Ordinary Level) program is a comprehensive 4-year secondary education curriculum designed to provide students with strong foundational knowledge across sciences, humanities, and practical skills.

A-Level Program

The A-Level (Advanced Level) program is a specialized 2-year curriculum for students who have completed O-Level. It offers in-depth study in chosen subjects to prepare students for university and professional careers.

Program Duration: 2 Years
